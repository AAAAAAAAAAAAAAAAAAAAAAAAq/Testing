FNF Chromatic Scale Generator

This tool automatically generates simple pitched chromatic scales from samples, providing an easier alternative to manual pitch-shifting methods for Friday Night Funkin' mod songs.

How to Use

1. Prepare Your Samples

  • Place your vowel or other sound samples in a single folder.
  • Ensure they are labeled as .wav files in numerical order, such as:
    • 1.wav
    • 2.wav
    • 3.wav
    • ... and so on, in the order you want them to appear in the chromatic scale.

2. Load Your Samples

  • Select the folder containing your .wav files.
  • Choose the following settings:
    • Starting Note (e.g., C4)
    • Octave Range
    • Gap Length (spacing between samples in the scale; cannot be 0)

3. Generate the Chromatic Scale

  • Click "Generate Chromatic" to create a fully-pitched chromatic scale. The output will be saved in the selected folder.
  • Optionally, you can choose to:
    • Dump the pitched samples (useful for making a soundfont).
    • Generate without pitching (creates a chromatic sequence using raw samples without pitch modification).

This tool simplifies the process of creating custom FNF chromatic scales, reducing the need for manual pitch adjustments and allowing for faster workflow in mod development.
